My Story

Passion for Performance

Skills in problem solving, team cooperation, discipline, and design have been developed over my 18 years as a performer. I have danced professionally with multiple ballet companies, received a B.A. in Dance at the University of California Irvine, and currently make magic in shows at the Disneyland Resort.

Exploring the Psyche

If my passion is performance, you might say my hobby is psychology. In my two years as a Behavioral Therapist, I learned Applied Behavioral Analysis (ABA) principles working with children who possess developmental delays. In my spare time, I often research Myers-Briggs’ (MBTI) personality types and habit-building techniques. I also enjoy taking psychology courses when life permits.

Growth in So Many Words

Greater understanding is my constant craving. Books, as well as the languages which make them, are the best cure, as I learned earning my B.A. in English at UC Irvine. I feel incomplete without a book in my goodreads’ “currently reading” list. I feel unfulfilled if I’m not learning some language, be it English, French, Dance, or CSS.
